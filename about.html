About Me

I'm currently in my second year of college, completing my Level 3 Extended BTEC Diploma in Creative Media Production. I have a strong foundation in media, with my experience prior to the course in editing and photography - my great grandad was a photographer for Ford for many years and taught me many tricks.

My best skill is editing; I have a good understanding of the process and like the creative freedom. I tend to opt for using Adobe After Effects, as it's the application I know best - and is very powerful.

When I'm not working on projects, I enjoy listening to music, coding and creating projects, or working.
